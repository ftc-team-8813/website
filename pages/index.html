About Us

We are a sixth year FIRST Tech Challenge team consisting of 14 students from Bettendorf, Iowa and the surrounding area. We are committed to creating competitive, industrially designed robots and spreading our passion for robotics across the globe. Over the past few years, we've worked to promote STEM in Iowa, Illinois, Alaska, Solomon Islands, Micronesia, Somalia, North Macedonia, Russia, and India.
