About Us

We are a ninth year FIRST Tech Challenge team consisting of 15 students from the Quad Cities, Iowa and Illinois. We are committed to creating competitive, industrially designed robots and spreading our passion for robotics across the globe. Over the past few years, we've worked to promote STEM in Iowa, Illinois, Alaska, Solomon Islands, Marshall Islands, Micronesia, Somalia, North Macedonia, Burundi, Fiji, Russia, and India.
